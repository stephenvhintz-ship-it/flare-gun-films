The Story

A historical science-fiction mystery where hidden histories, powerful legacies, and the world’s unexplained wonders collide.

Antiquarian and adventurer Tawny Bennett is drawn into a world of mysteries alongside Doctor Willow, Madame C. J. Walker’s personal physician. The film brings real figures and imagined possibilities together in a story built around discovery, identity, and what survives across generations.
