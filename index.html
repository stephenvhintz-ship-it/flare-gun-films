About

Flare Gun Films

Flare Gun Films develops bold, human, cinematic stories with commercial potential and cultural weight. Founded by writer-producer Stephen Hintz, the company focuses on hidden histories, unlikely heroes, legacy stories, and projects that illuminate what often goes unseen.
