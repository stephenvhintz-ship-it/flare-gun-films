About

Flare Gun Films

Flare Gun Films develops bold, human stories with commercial potential and cultural weight. Founded by writer-producer Stephen Hintz, the company focuses on elevated independent features, literary IP, and true stories exploring identity, family, race, legacy, survival, and redemption.

Based in Arkansas and working globally, Flare Gun Films develops projects across film, television, and publishing.
